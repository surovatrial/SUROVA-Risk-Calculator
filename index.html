SUROVA Risk Calculator for ovarian cancer

This tool helps estimate the risk of mortality in ovarian cancer patients based on clinical factors. The risk score calculation uses the beta coefficients of the dichotomized independent variables specified below, derived from a Cox regression model built with the non-asian patients with high-grade ovarian cancer of the SUROVA dataset (N=2416).
